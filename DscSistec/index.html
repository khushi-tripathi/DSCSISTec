What is DSC?

Google collaborates with Developer Student Clubs (DSC). Developer Student Clubs are university-based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies.

DSC provides a group of opportunities for students that helps to enhance their technical and non-technical skills. It is the platform in which you interact with new people and do work with them as a team that enhances your skill set, magnifies your early abilities, and increases your experience.
